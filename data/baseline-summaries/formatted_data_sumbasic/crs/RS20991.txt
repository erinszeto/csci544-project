action plans embody the strategies employed to achieve goals .

the office's strategic plan should not only identify specific steps , but also the person ( s ) ( including the member ) responsible for each step .

it is also useful to include deadlines for completing action on each step .

periodic meetings to review progress on the plan may prove useful in keeping the project on track .

usually each person in the office , whether they have specific responsibility for parts of the plan , should be provided a copy of the plan .
