in some instances , an excise tax is imposed on certain corporate insiders who benefit from a corporate inversion .

specifically , such individuals are subject to a 15% excise tax on the value of any stock compensation held by or for the benefit of the individual or his / her family during the specified time period .

the specified time period is the 12-month period beginning on the date which is six months prior to the date that the corporation inverts .

individuals who are subject to this tax are certain beneficial owners , officers , and directors of the corporation or any of its affiliates .

there are also consequences for inverted corporations under federal procurement law .
